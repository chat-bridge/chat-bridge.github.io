Don't litter your phone contacts

✨ whithout creating a contact ✨

We text different people on WhatsApp all the time. Local landlords, Airbnb hosts, delivery people, restaurants, tutors, beauty salons, plumbers, electricians. In our own city and when we travel to another city and country.

All the time!

And to write to them, we create, and create, and create new contacts in our phone. Dozens and hundreds of unnecessary contacts turn your personal phone into the yellow pages.

It's time to stop it. Stop your phones from turning into a contact dump.

Just text a message, enter a phone number and ChatBridge will open a chat with that person without you having to add them to your contacts.

Magic!
